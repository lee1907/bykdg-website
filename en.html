About us

A small studio with a focus on craft

Bykdg was founded by Ali Büyükdağ as a platform to ship independently-developed mobile applications. We work under a single developer identity on Google Play and Apple App Store, and we bring each product to life with the same attention to detail — from interface polish down to backend reliability.

Our apps share a common philosophy: solve one real problem well, protect user privacy, and keep the experience simple. We use Flutter for a unified codebase across iOS and Android, and Firebase for authentication, analytics, and crash reporting.

What we believe

Values that shape every release

We don't ship for vanity metrics. Each release goes through a quality bar shaped by these principles.

🔒

Privacy first

User data stays on-device when possible. KVKK and GDPR compliant. Account deletion in seconds. No third-party trackers we don't control.

⚡

Performance over features

Smooth scrolling, fast launches, no jank. Every app runs offline-first when feasible. Battery and bandwidth conscious by default.

🎯

One clear purpose

Each app solves a specific everyday problem instead of trying to be everything. Focus over feature creep.

🤝

Direct support

Every email gets a human reply. We read crash reports, ship patches fast, and respect user feedback in roadmaps.
